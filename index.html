VICTOR TEMPRANO

Victor Temprano creates worlds of code, history, and imagination in Santa Cruz de Oleiros, Spain, and Vancouver, Canada. A self-taught programmer with a M.A. in Buddhist Studies, his interests touch on subjects across time, space, and cultures.

He is the founder of Native-Land.ca, the CEO of Mapster Technology Inc, and an independent digital humanist scholar.

He is a recent graduate of Simon Fraser University's Writer's Studio where he specialized in fiction and non-fiction.

He is currently working on a wide range of writing projects and digital histories. He was raised in Kelowna, British Columbia.
